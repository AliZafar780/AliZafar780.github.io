About

I'm a security researcher and AI systems architect driven by an insatiable curiosity about how systems work—and how they can be broken. My journey spans cybersecurity, machine learning, and digital photography, each discipline informing the others in unexpected ways.

In security, I believe the best defense comes from understanding offense. In AI, I focus on building systems that are not just powerful, but also secure and interpretable. Through photography, I capture moments that tell stories about the intersection of technology and human experience.

Every project I undertake follows a simple philosophy: understand the underlying mechanics, identify the weaknesses, and build something stronger than what existed before.
